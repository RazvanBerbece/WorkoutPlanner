Have you ever got to the gym and realised that you don't remember what you were supposed to train today ? (Most probably not). But worry no more, as the Workout Planner has been created to aid you in your routine. Before going to gym, make your own list of exercises, reps, and you'll be given an approximate amount of time in which you should finish your training. Last, but not least, you won't forget what to train and the order you want to do so !
